# Future Grooming Sensitivity Notes

This document tracks follow-up ideas that are not part of the current locked preprocessing or main unblinded result set.

## Brief receive reclassification sensitivity

Potential issue:

- Because only Hedy was behaviorally labeled, very brief Hooke-to-Hedy contact was sometimes coded as `Groom receive`.
- In hindsight, some of those very short `Groom receive` bouts may reflect Hooke soliciting grooming from Hedy rather than actually grooming her.

Potential future sensitivity rule:

- Reclassify any `Groom receive` bout with duration `< 3 s` as a derived solicitation-style category such as `groom_solicited`, or fold it into `Groom solicit` for a sensitivity analysis.

Why this matters:

- The main session-level result currently looks strongest for reduced `groom_give` under DCZ.
- The weaker `groom_receive` side of the story may be more vulnerable to this coding ambiguity.
- The directional within-episode follow-up is especially sensitive to this issue because brief `Groom receive` bouts can create apparent `receive`-initiated episodes that may actually be solicitation-driven.

How to use this note later:

- Treat this as a future sensitivity analysis, not as a replacement for the current main pipeline.
- If revisited, compare the current component results against the reclassified version and ask whether the apparent increase in `groom_receive` survives.
